About National Academy

National Academy is dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, strong moral values, leadership qualities and holistic development. We provide a safe and inspiring learning environment where students grow into confident and responsible citizens.


Located at Shahnewazpur, Taraiya (Saran), Bihar, our institution strives to create future-ready learners through modern education and co-curricular activities.
